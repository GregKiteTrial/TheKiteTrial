The Kite Trial: Investigating A Novel Treatment for Bipolar Depression

The University of Melbourne is researching an innovative treatment for individuals experiencing bipolar depression.

Your involvement in this research could contribute to potential improvements in mental health care for people with bipolar disorder.

About the Trial

If you, or someone you know, are navigating the challenges of bipolar depression and are interested in participating in a clinical trial that offers a novel treatment approach, we invite you to learn more by filling out the form below or talking to your healthcare provider.
